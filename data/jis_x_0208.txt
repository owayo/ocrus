#

# - ASCII printable: U+0021..U+007E
# - CJK Symbols and Punctuation: U+3000..U+303F
# - Hiragana: U+3041..U+3096
# - Katakana: U+30A1..U+30FA
# - CJK Unified Ideographs: U+4E00..U+9FFF
# - Fullwidth Forms: U+FF01..U+FF5E
